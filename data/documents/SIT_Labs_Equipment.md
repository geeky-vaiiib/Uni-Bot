## Lab Rules

### General Rules
1. Wear lab coat (where required)
2. No food or drinks
3. Follow safety guidelines
4. Report damaged equipment
5. Maintain cleanliness
6. Log in/out in register

### Computer Lab Rules
1. Don't install unauthorized software
2. Don't change system settings
3. Save work on personal drives
4. Log off after use
5. No gaming

### Safety Rules
1. Know emergency exits
2. Fire extinguisher locations
3. First aid kit in each lab
4. Report accidents immediately
5. Follow faculty instructions
