# SIT Food and Dining - Complete Guide

## Overview
SIT offers one of the unique food experiences among engineering colleges through the Siddaganga Math Dasoha program - providing FREE food to all students. This guide covers all food options on campus.

---

## Dasoha - Free Food Program

### About Dasoha
Dasoha is a centuries-old tradition of Siddaganga Math, meaning "selfless service through food." Started by the late Shivakumara Swamiji, this program feeds over 10,000 people daily.

**Key Features**:
- 100% FREE for all SIT students
- Nutritious vegetarian meals
- No discrimination - open to all
- Part of Siddaganga Math's service tradition

**Annual Savings**: Students save approximately ₹30,000 - ₹40,000 per year on food!

## FAQs

**Q: Is the free food (Dasoha) really free?**
A: Yes, absolutely free for all SIT students and visitors to Siddaganga Math.

**Q: Can I skip Dasoha and eat only at canteen?**
A: Yes, it's your choice. Dasoha is optional.

**Q: Is non-veg food available?**
A: No, entire campus is vegetarian only.

**Q: Can I order food from outside?**
A: Limited - delivery to gate, hostelers need permission.

**Q: What if I have food allergies?**
A: Inform staff in advance. Canteens can accommodate basic requests.

**Q: Are there midnight snacks available?**
A: Not officially. You can keep packaged snacks in room.
