# SIT Student Services - Complete Guide

## Overview
This document covers all administrative services and processes available to students at SIT, including document requests, leave procedures, and support services.

---

## ID Card Services

### New Student ID Card

**Process**:
1. Submit passport photos (2) to department
2. Fill ID card form
3. Biometric capture at Admin block
4. Collect ID card within 7 days

**Details on ID Card**:
- Student name and photo
- USN (University Serial Number)
- Branch and admission year
- Blood group
- Barcode for library and attendance

### Lost ID Card

**Steps**:
1. File FIR at local police station (optional but recommended)
2. Submit application to HOD
3. Pay duplicate ID card fee: ₹100
4. Submit passport photo
5. Collect new ID within 5 days

### ID Card Renewal
- Not required (valid for entire course)
- Replace only if damaged or details change

## Certificates and Documents

### Bonafide Certificate

**Purpose**: Proof of enrollment for various purposes

**Process**:
1. Submit application at department office
2. State purpose (travel, visa, scholarship, etc.)
3. Processing time: 2-3 working days
4. Collect from HOD office

**Fee**: ₹50 per certificate

### Study Certificate

**Purpose**: Detailed enrollment information

**Contents**: Course details, duration, USN, current semester

**Process**: Same as Bonafide certificate

**Fee**: ₹50 per certificate

### Character Certificate

**Purpose**: Required for jobs, higher studies

**Process**:
1. Apply after completion of course
2. No disciplinary record check
3. Issued by Principal

**Fee**: ₹100

### Transfer Certificate (TC)

**Purpose**: Leaving the institution

**Process**:
1. Submit TC application form
2. Clear all library dues
3. Clear hostel dues
4. No Fee dues (obtain no-dues certificate)
5. Return ID card
6. Processing: 7-10 days

**Fee**: ₹200

### Migration Certificate

**Purpose**: For joining other university

**Process**:
1. Apply with TC
2. Provide admission letter of new institution
3. VTU migration process

**Fee**: ₹500 (VTU charges additional)

## Academic Documents

### Marks Cards / Grade Cards

**During Course**:
- Issued by VTU after each semester
- Collect from Exam section
- Digital copies available on VTU website

**Consolidated Marks Card**:
- After course completion
- All semester marks in one document
- Fee: ₹500

### Degree Certificate

**Collection**:
- After Convocation ceremony
- Or collect from VTU Belagavi
- Fee: Included in convocation fee

### Provisional Certificate

**Purpose**: Temporary document before degree issue

**Eligibility**: After passing all subjects

**Process**:
1. Apply at exam section
2. All dues cleared
3. Processing: 5 days

**Fee**: ₹200

## Leave Application

### Casual Leave (Class)

**Process**:
1. Submit leave letter to class teacher
2. Parent letter for >3 days
3. Get signature from HOD for >5 days

**Limit**: Maintain 85% attendance

### Medical Leave

**Documents Required**:
- Leave application
- Medical certificate from registered doctor
- Hospital records (if hospitalized)

**Process**:
1. Submit application within 3 days of resuming
2. Attach medical documents
3. HOD approval required

**Note**: Medical leave may be considered for attendance shortage

### On-Duty Leave

**Eligible Activities**:
- Workshops and seminars
- Competitions (technical/cultural)
- NSS/NCC camps
- Sports events

**Process**:
1. Prior permission from HOD
2. Letter from organizing institution
3. Submit OD form
4. Get signed by faculty coordinator

### Hostel Leave

See Hostel Guide for detailed leave procedures.

## Transport Services

### College Bus Pass

**Process**:
1. Apply at transport section
2. Select route
3. Pay annual/semester fee
4. Collect bus pass

### Routes Available
- Tumakuru city routes (5+)
- Nearby towns (Kunigal, Tiptur, etc.)

### Bus Timings
- Morning pickup: 7:30 AM - 8:15 AM
- Evening drop: 5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

## Convocation

### Registration

**Process**:
1. Register on VTU portal
2. Pay convocation fee: ₹2,000
3. Collect robe and cap
4. Attend ceremony

### In Absentia

**If unable to attend**:
1. Apply for in-absentia
2. Degree sent by post
3. Additional fee: ₹500

### What You Receive
- Degree Certificate
- Consolidated Marks Card
- Convocation Medal (if eligible)
