## How to Reach SIT

### From Bangalore

#### By Bus
- **KSRTC Buses**: Frequent services from Majestic Bus Stand
- **Private Buses**: Available from Peenya, Yeshwanthpur
- **Duration**: 1.5-2 hours
- **Fare**: ₹80-150
- **Frequency**: Every 15-30 minutes

#### By Train
- **Tumkur Railway Station**: 4 km from SIT
- **Trains from Bangalore**:
  - Several passenger and express trains
  - Duration: 1-1.5 hours
  - Fare: ₹50-100
- **Auto from station**: ₹50-80 to campus

#### By Car
- **Route**: NH4 (Bangalore-Pune Highway)
- **Distance**: 70 km
- **Toll**: Approximately ₹100-150
- **Time**: 1.5 hours (non-peak)

### From Airport

#### From Kempegowda International Airport (Bangalore)
- **Distance**: 100 km
- **By Car**: 2-2.5 hours
- **By Bus**: Take bus to Majestic, then to Tumkur
- **Approximate Cost**: 
  - Taxi: ₹2000-2500
  - Bus: ₹200-300 total

## Local Transport in Tumkur

### Auto Rickshaws
- Available throughout city
- SIT to Bus Stand: ₹40-60
- SIT to Railway Station: ₹50-80
- Meter not commonly used, negotiate fare

### City Buses
- Limited frequency
- Very economical
- Fare: ₹10-20

### Cab Services
- Ola/Uber: Available but limited
- Local taxi services
- Book in advance for reliability
