# SIT Campus Life and Facilities

## Campus Overview

Siddaganga Institute of Technology is located in Tumakuru, approximately 70 km from Bangalore. The campus is established under the Siddaganga Education Society, which is associated with the historic Siddaganga Math.

**Campus Area**: Approximately 45 acres
**Location**: B.H. Road (Bangalore-Honnavar Highway), Tumakuru

---

- **Total Hostels**: 8 blocks
- **Capacity**: 3000+ students
- **Room Types**: 
  - Triple sharing
  - Four sharing
  - Six sharing
- **Facilities**:
  - Attached/Common bathrooms
  - Hot water facility (morning hours)
  - Wi-Fi connectivity
  - Common TV room

### Girls Hostels
- **Total Hostels**: 3 blocks
- **Capacity**: 800+ students
- **Features**:
  - Enhanced security (24/7 wardens)
  - CCTV surveillance
  - Visitor restrictions
  - Separate entrance

## Mess and Food

### Central Mess (Dasoha - Free Food!)
One of the unique features of SIT is the **Siddaganga Math Dasoha** - a free food program inspired by the tradition of Siddaganga Math.

- **Breakfast**: 7:00 AM - 8:30 AM
- **Lunch**: 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM
- **Snacks**: 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM
- **Dinner**: 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM

**Menu**: Nutritious vegetarian South Indian food
- Rice, Sambar, Rasam
- Roti, Vegetable curry
- Curd rice
- Special items on festivals

### Paid Canteens
- **Main Canteen**: Near administrative block
- **Department Canteens**: In each department
- **Food Court**: Multiple cuisine options
- **Juice Centers**: Fresh fruit juices
