## How to Apply

### SIT Scholarships
1. **Automatic**: Rank-based scholarships applied at admission
2. **Merit Scholarships**: Submit previous year marksheets to scholarship cell

### Government Scholarships (SSP Karnataka)

**Step-by-Step Process**:
1. Register at ssp.karnataka.gov.in
2. Fill application form
3. Upload documents:
   - Aadhar Card
   - Caste Certificate
   - Income Certificate
   - Fee Receipt
   - Bank Passbook
   - Previous year marks
4. Submit and track application
5. Institute verification
6. Disbursement to bank account

**Deadline**: Usually September-October each year

### National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

**Step-by-Step Process**:
1. Register at scholarships.gov.in
2. Complete profile with Aadhar
3. Select applicable scholarship
4. Upload documents
5. Submit application
6. Institute verification
7. District verification
8. Fund disbursement

## FAQs

**Q: Can I apply for multiple scholarships?**
A: Yes, but you can receive only one government scholarship at a time. Private scholarships can be combined.

**Q: What if my scholarship is rejected?**
A: Check rejection reason, correct documents, and reapply if possible.

**Q: When will I receive the scholarship amount?**
A: Usually 2-3 months after institute verification.

**Q: Is there a minimum attendance requirement?**
A: Yes, 75% attendance is typically required for scholarship continuation.
