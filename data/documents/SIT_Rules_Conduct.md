# SIT Rules, Conduct, and Discipline

## General Code of Conduct

### For All Students

1. **Identity Card**
   - ID card must be worn at all times on campus
   - Present when asked by security/faculty
   - Report lost ID immediately
   - Replacement fee: ₹100

2. **Dress Code**
   - Formal dress is mandatory
   - Boys: Formal shirts, trousers, formal shoes
   - Girls: Salwar kameez, sarees, or formal western (no sleeveless)
   - No shorts, sleeveless, or revealing clothes
   - Friday: Relaxed dress code (casual allowed)
   - Lab: Appropriate lab coat/apron

3. **Attendance**
   - Minimum 85% attendance required
   - Attendance taken at start of each class
   - Late entry: 15 minutes grace, then marked absent
   - Bunking classes is a disciplinary offense

4. **Punctuality**
   - Classes start at 9:00 AM sharp
   - Late comers may be denied entry
   - Assembly/events: Report on time

5. **Mobile Phones**
   - Switched off during classes
   - Not allowed during exams
   - No recording without permission

## Academic Integrity

### Anti-Cheating Policy

1. **During Examinations**
   - No electronic devices
   - No communication with others
   - No unauthorized materials
   - Follow invigilator instructions
   - Sit in assigned seats only

2. **Malpractice Consequences**
   | Offense | Punishment |
   |---------|-----------|
   | Carrying mobile | Exam cancelled |
   | Possession of chits | Subject cancelled |
   | Copying | Semester cancelled |
   | Impersonation | Debarred from university |
   | Physical assault | Expelled |

### Plagiarism Policy
- All assignments checked for plagiarism
- Maximum similarity: 20%
- First offense: Warning
- Second offense: Zero marks
- Repeated offense: Disciplinary action

## Anti-Ragging Policy

### Zero Tolerance for Ragging

#### What Constitutes Ragging?
- Any act that causes physical or psychological harm
- Teasing, abusing, or insulting juniors
- Forcing to do anything against will
- Verbal or physical harassment
- Discrimination based on caste, region, language

#### Consequences
- Suspension from classes
- Expulsion from hostel
- Debarment from exams
- Withholding results
- Rustication from college
- Criminal prosecution under law

#### How to Report
- **Toll-free Helpline**: 1800-180-5522
- **UGC Anti-Ragging Helpline**: www.antiragging.in
- **College Committee**: Principal's office
- **Complaint Box**: At security gate
- **Email**: antiragging@sit.ac.in

#### Anti-Ragging Squad
- Regular hostel inspections
- Random campus patrols
- Quick response to complaints
- Awareness programs

## Hostel Rules

### General Rules

1. **Timings**
   - Entry: 6:00 AM
   - In-time (Boys): 9:00 PM
   - In-time (Girls): 8:00 PM
   - Extended permission: Apply in advance

2. **Visitors**
   - Visitors allowed only in designated areas
   - Prior permission required
   - Visitors' register mandatory
   - No overnight visitors

3. **Leave and Outing**
   - Leave application to warden
   - Parents' consent for overnight leave
   - Sign out/in register mandatory
   - Weekend leave: Apply by Thursday

4. **Room Rules**
   - Keep room clean and tidy
   - No cooking in rooms
   - No electrical appliances without permission
   - No damage to property
   - Lights off by 11:00 PM (except exam time)

5. **Prohibited Items**
   - Alcohol
   - Drugs and tobacco
   - Weapons
   - Inflammable materials
   - Pets
   - Unauthorized electrical items

### Mess Rules
- Fixed meal timings
- No wasting food
- Use only assigned dining halls
- Guest meals: Inform in advance
- Feedback/complaints to mess committee

### Hostel Fines
| Violation | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| Late entry | ₹50 first time |
| Repeated late entry | ₹100 + warning |
| Unauthorized absence | ₹200 + parents informed |
| Damage to property | Cost of repair |
| Violation of rules | As decided by warden |

## Laboratory Rules

### General Lab Safety

1. **Before Entering Lab**
   - Wear appropriate attire
   - Lab coat where required
   - No loose clothing/jewelry
   - Safety glasses where required

2. **During Lab Session**
   - Follow instructor guidelines
   - Don't operate equipment without permission
   - Report malfunctions immediately
   - No food or drinks
   - Keep workspace clean

3. **Equipment Care**
   - Handle equipment carefully
   - Return to original position
   - Report any damage
   - Don't remove from lab

4. **Computer Lab Specific**
   - No gaming
   - No unauthorized software installation
   - No virus-carrying devices
   - Log off after use
   - No hacking attempts

## Examination Rules

### Before Exam
- Collect hall ticket from department
- Verify all details
- Report discrepancies immediately
- Clear all dues

### During Exam
- Arrive 30 minutes early
- Carry ID card and hall ticket
- Sit only in assigned seat
- No communication with others
- Raise hand for any needs
- No leaving before 1 hour

### Prohibited in Exam Hall
- Mobile phones
- Smartwatches
- Bluetooth devices
- Bags (keep at designated area)
- Written notes
- Programmable calculators (unless permitted)

### After Exam
- Stop writing when time is called
- Submit answer booklet to invigilator
- Don't take question paper if not allowed
- Leave hall quietly

## Disciplinary Procedures

### Reporting Violations
1. Faculty/staff report to HOD
2. HOD reports to Dean/Principal
3. Disciplinary committee convenes
4. Student given opportunity to explain
5. Appropriate action taken

### Disciplinary Committee
- Principal (Chairman)
- Dean (Student Welfare)
- Concerned HOD
- Senior faculty members

### Types of Punishments
1. **Warning**: Verbal/written
2. **Fine**: Monetary penalty
3. **Detention**: Extra work/attendance
4. **Suspension**: Temporary removal
5. **Rustication**: Permanent removal

### Appeal Process
- Written appeal to Principal within 7 days
- Appeal Committee reviews
- Final decision communicated

## Grievance Redressal

### How to File Grievance

### Grievance Cell
