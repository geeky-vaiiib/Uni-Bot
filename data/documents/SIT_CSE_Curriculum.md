# SIT CSE Curriculum - Semester-wise Subjects

## Overview
The Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) program at SIT follows the VTU curriculum with autonomous modifications. This document covers all 8 semesters of the B.E. CSE program.

**Program Duration**: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
**Total Credits**: 160-170 Credits
**Scheme**: 2021 Scheme (Current)
