## Placement Process

### Timeline
| Month | Activity |
|-------|----------|
| July-August | Pre-placement talks |
| September-October | Aptitude training |
| October onwards | Major company drives |
| December-January | Mass recruiters |
| February-April | Off-campus drives |

### Selection Process (Typical)
1. **Online Test**: Aptitude + Technical + Coding
2. **Technical Interview**: 1-2 rounds
3. **HR Interview**: Final round
4. **Results**: Same day or next day

### Eligibility Criteria (General)
- No active backlogs at time of placement
- Minimum 60% aggregate (some companies require 70%)
- Good communication skills
- No disciplinary issues

## Tips for Students

### To Maximize Placement Chances
1. Start coding practice from 2nd year
2. Build strong fundamentals
3. Complete at least 2 internships
4. Work on real projects
5. Participate in hackathons
6. Maintain good academics (>7 CGPA)
7. Develop communication skills
8. Create strong LinkedIn profile
9. Contribute to open source
10. Network with seniors and alumni

### Resources Recommended
- LeetCode, HackerRank, CodeChef (for coding)
- GeeksforGeeks (for DSA)
- InterviewBit (for interview prep)
- Glassdoor (for company reviews)
- LinkedIn Learning (for skills)
