# SIT Student Activities, Clubs, and Events

## Technical Clubs and Organizations

### IEEE Student Branch - SIT
- **About**: Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers student chapter
- **Activities**: Technical workshops, paper presentations, hackathons
- **Membership**: Open to all engineering students
- **Benefits**: Access to IEEE resources, certifications, global network
- **Contact**: ieee@sit.ac.in

### Google Developer Student Club (GDSC)
- **About**: Google-supported tech community
- **Focus Areas**: Android, Web, Cloud, Machine Learning
- **Activities**: 
  - Weekly coding sessions
  - Google technologies workshops
  - Solution challenges
  - Study jams
- **Eligibility**: All students can join
- **Selection**: Application and interview process for core team

### Coding Club
- **About**: Competitive programming community
- **Activities**:
  - Weekly contests
  - DSA practice sessions
  - Interview preparation
  - Hackathon participation
- **Platforms Used**: LeetCode, CodeForces, HackerRank
- **Achievements**: Multiple ICPC qualifiers

### Robotics Club
- **About**: Building and programming robots
- **Activities**:
  - Robot building workshops
  - Competitions (Robocon, Techfest)
  - Drone projects
  - IoT projects
- **Facilities**: Dedicated robotics lab

### Cybersecurity Club
- **About**: Ethical hacking and security
- **Activities**:
  - CTF competitions
  - Security workshops
  - Bug bounty hunting
  - Awareness programs

### Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell (IEC)
- **About**: Fostering startup culture
- **Activities**:
  - Startup mentorship
  - Pitch competitions
  - Funding guidance
  - Industry connects
- **Incubation**: Support for student startups

### ACM Student Chapter
- **About**: Association for Computing Machinery chapter
- **Focus**: Research, coding, technical papers

## Major Annual Events

### Siddaganga Utsav (Cultural Fest)
- **When**: February (typically)
- **Duration**: 3 days
- **Activities**:
  - Celebrity performances
  - Dance competitions
  - Music contests
  - Fashion show
  - Gaming events
  - Food stalls
- **Footfall**: 5000+ participants

### Technical Symposium (Tech Fest)
- **When**: January-February
- **Duration**: 2-3 days
- **Events**:
  - Paper presentations
  - Project exhibitions
  - Coding contests
  - Hackathons
  - Robotics challenges
  - Guest lectures
- **Prizes**: Cash prizes and certificates

### Spoorthi (Sports Meet)
- **When**: December-January
- **Duration**: 1 week
- **Sports**: All major indoor/outdoor sports
- **Levels**: Inter-class, Inter-department
- **Highlights**: Opening ceremony, finals

### Freshers Day
- **When**: August
- **For**: First-year students
- **Activities**: Talent show, introductions, cultural programs

### Graduation Day
- **When**: June
- **Ceremony**: Formal convocation
- **Chief Guest**: Eminent personality
- **Awards**: Gold medals, rank certificates

### Engineers Day (September 15)
- Celebrates Sir M. Visvesvaraya's birthday
- Technical talks and activities

### Independence Day & Republic Day
- Flag hoisting ceremony
- Cultural programs
- NCC parade

### Founders Day
- Remembering Shivakumara Swamiji
- Special programs

## How to Join Clubs

### Process
1. Attend orientation sessions (first month of college)
2. Fill registration forms
3. Some clubs have selection process
4. Active participation required

### Benefits of Joining Clubs
- Skill development
- Networking opportunities
- Resume building
- Fun and relaxation
- Prizes and recognition
- Industry exposure
