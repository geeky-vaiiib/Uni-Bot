## Room Facilities

### Standard Room Amenities
- **Beds**: Metal cots with mattresses
- **Storage**: Individual steel almirah/locker
- **Study**: Shared study table and chairs
- **Fans**: Ceiling fans (2 per room)
- **Lights**: Tube lights and study lamps
- **Power**: 2-3 electrical outlets per student

### Common Amenities Per Block
- **Bathrooms**: Common bathrooms (ratio 1:8)
- **Hot Water**: Geyser available (morning hours)
- **Drinking Water**: RO purified water
- **WiFi**: 24/7 internet connectivity
- **TV Room**: Common TV with DTH
- **Reading Room**: Quiet study area
- **Indoor Games**: Carrom, Chess, TT

### Additional Facilities
- **Laundry**: Dhobi service available
- **Ironing**: Iron box on each floor
- **First Aid**: Medical kit at warden office
- **Emergency**: Fire extinguishers, emergency exits

## Hostel Rules and Regulations

### General Rules
1. **Identity**: Always carry ID card within campus
2. **Attendance**: Mandatory attendance during roll call
3. **Cleanliness**: Keep rooms clean and tidy
4. **Silence**: Maintain silence during study hours (10 PM - 6 AM)
5. **Visitors**: Guests must register at gate

### Prohibited Items
- ❌ Alcohol and tobacco products
- ❌ Drugs and narcotics
- ❌ Weapons or sharp objects
- ❌ Electrical appliances (heaters, cookers)
- ❌ Pets
- ❌ Vehicles inside hostel area

### Leave Procedures

#### Day Outing
- Available on weekends and holidays
- Apply through hostel warden
- Return by 6:00 PM
- Parent's consent required for juniors

#### Weekend Leave
- Submit application 2 days prior
- Parent verification call required
- Maximum 2 weekends per month (first years)

#### Long Leave (Vacations)
- Apply through department and hostel
- Surrender room keys
- Valuable belongings in safe custody

### Penalties for Violations
| Violation | First Offense | Repeat |
|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Late return | Warning | Fine ₹500 |
| Unauthorized absence | Parents informed | Fine ₹1000 |
| Ragging | Suspension | Expulsion |
| Alcohol/Smoking | Suspension | Expulsion |
| Damage to property | Cost + ₹500 | Expulsion |

## Hostel Admission Process

### Eligibility
- Full-time SIT students only
- Priority to outstation students
- Distance criteria: Minimum 30 km from Tumakuru

### Required Documents
1. Hostel admission form
2. College admission letter
3. Parent's consent letter
4. Medical fitness certificate
5. Passport size photos (4)
6. ID proof (Aadhar)
7. Fee payment receipt

### Allocation Criteria
1. Distance from hometown
2. First-come-first-served
3. Senior students get preference for better rooms

## Tips for New Hostelers

### What to Bring
✅ Bedsheets and pillow
✅ Bucket and mug
✅ Personal toiletries
✅ Clothes for all seasons
✅ Study materials
✅ Lock for almirah
✅ Photos (passport size)
✅ Extension cord (1 only)

### What NOT to Bring
❌ Expensive jewelry
❌ Large cash amounts
❌ Cooking appliances
❌ Multiple electronic devices

### Adjustment Tips
1. Introduce yourself to roommates
2. Establish ground rules early
3. Respect quiet hours
4. Keep valuables locked
5. Participate in hostel activities
6. Report issues to warden promptly
