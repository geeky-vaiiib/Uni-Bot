Siddaganga Institute of Technology offers affordable education compared to other private engineering colleges. Fees are subject to revision by VTU/State Government annually.

**Note**: All fees mentioned are approximate for the 2025-26 academic year. Actual fees may vary based on government notifications.

## Fee Concessions and Discounts

### Merit-based Concessions
- KCET Rank within 1000: 100% tuition fee waiver (1st year)
- KCET Rank 1001-2500: 50% tuition fee waiver (1st year)
- KCET Rank 2501-5000: 25% tuition fee waiver (1st year)

### Category-based Concessions
- SC/ST students: Government fee reimbursement
- OBC students: Partial government support
- Economically weaker sections: Various government schemes

### Sibling Discount
- Second sibling studying at SIT: 10% discount on tuition

## Refund Policy

### Before Academic Year Starts
- Tuition Fee: 90% refund
- Caution Deposit: 100% refund
- Admission Fee: Non-refundable

### Within 15 Days of Classes
- Tuition Fee: 75% refund

### Within 30 Days of Classes
- Tuition Fee: 50% refund

### After 30 Days
- No refund except deposits
