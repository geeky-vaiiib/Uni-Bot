# SIT History, About, and Leadership

## About Siddaganga Institute of Technology

### Establishment and History
Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT) was established in **1963** under the visionary leadership of **His Holiness Dr. Sri Sri Sri Shivakumara Swamiji** of Siddaganga Math. The institute was founded with the noble aim of providing quality technical education to students, especially those from rural and economically weaker sections of society.

### The Founder - Walking God of Karnataka
**Dr. Sri Sri Sri Shivakumara Swamiji (1907-2019)**
- Fondly known as the "Walking God" and "Nadedaadaga Devaru"
- Established over 132 educational institutions
- Fed lakhs of people daily through Dasoha (free food program)
- Awarded Padma Bhushan in 2015
- Lived for 111 years, dedicated entirely to education and service
- His vision: "Education for all, regardless of caste, creed, or economic status"

### Current Leadership
**Present Pontiff**: Sri Sri Sri Dr. Siddalinga Mahaswamiji
- Continuing the legacy of service and education
- Modernizing infrastructure while preserving values
- Expanding research and industry collaborations

## Siddaganga Math - The Parent Organization

### About Siddaganga Math
- One of the most revered religious institutions in Karnataka
- Over 600 years of history
- Located in Tumakuru, Karnataka
- Known for Dasoha - feeding thousands daily for free

### Educational Institutions Under Siddaganga Math
1. Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT)
2. Siddaganga College of Pharmacy
3. Siddaganga Law College
4. Siddaganga First Grade College
5. Numerous schools and PU colleges
6. Over 132 institutions total

### Dasoha - The Free Food Tradition
- Started by Shivakumara Swamiji
- Serves over 10,000 meals daily
- No discrimination based on caste, creed, or religion
- Available to all SIT students
- Inspired by the Lingayat tradition of service
