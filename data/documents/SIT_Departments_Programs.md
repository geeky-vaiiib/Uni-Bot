# SIT Departments and Programs

## Undergraduate Programs (B.E./B.Arch) - 4 Years

### Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
- **Intake**: 180 seats
- **CET Code**: E16-CS
- **Annual Fee**: ₹1,25,000 (approximate)
- **Placement Rate**: 95%+
- **Top Recruiters**: Microsoft, Amazon, Google, Infosys, TCS, Wipro
- **Key Subjects**: Data Structures, Algorithms, Database Systems, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Machine Learning, Web Development
- **Labs**: Programming Lab, DBMS Lab, Networks Lab, AI/ML Lab

### CSE - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
- **Intake**: 60 seats
- **Focus Areas**: Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Robotics
- **Special Features**: GPU Computing Lab, Industry partnerships with AI companies

### CSE - Cyber Security
- **Intake**: 60 seats
- **Focus Areas**: Network Security, Cryptography, Ethical Hacking, Digital Forensics
- **Certifications**: Students can pursue CEH, CISSP preparation courses

### Information Science & Engineering (ISE)
- **Intake**: 120 seats
- **CET Code**: E16-IS
- **Focus**: Software Engineering, Data Analytics, Cloud Computing
- **Placement Rate**: 90%+

### Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
- **Intake**: 120 seats
- **CET Code**: E16-EC
- **Key Areas**: VLSI Design, Embedded Systems, Signal Processing, IoT
- **Labs**: Microprocessor Lab, Communication Lab, VLSI Lab

### Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)
- **Intake**: 60 seats
- **CET Code**: E16-EE
- **Focus Areas**: Power Systems, Control Systems, Renewable Energy

### Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering
- **Intake**: 30 seats
- **Focus Areas**: Industrial Automation, Process Control, Biomedical Instrumentation

### Mechanical Engineering
- **Intake**: 120 seats
- **CET Code**: E16-ME
- **Key Areas**: Thermal Engineering, Manufacturing, CAD/CAM, Robotics
- **Labs**: Workshop, CAD Lab, Thermal Lab, Materials Testing Lab

### Civil Engineering
- **Intake**: 60 seats
- **CET Code**: E16-CE
- **Focus Areas**: Structural Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Transportation

### Chemical Engineering
- **Intake**: 30 seats
- **Focus Areas**: Process Engineering, Petrochemicals, Pollution Control

### Biotechnology
- **Intake**: 30 seats
- **Focus Areas**: Genetic Engineering, Bioinformatics, Pharmaceutical Biotechnology

### Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
- **Intake**: 60 seats
- **Focus Areas**: Big Data Analytics, Statistical Learning, Data Visualization

### Architecture (B.Arch) - 5 Years
- **Intake**: 40 seats
- **CET Code**: E16-AR
- **Focus Areas**: Architectural Design, Urban Planning, Sustainable Architecture
- **Special Requirements**: NATA score required

## Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech/MBA/MCA) - 2 Years

### M.Tech Programs
1. **Computer Science & Engineering** - 18 seats
2. **VLSI Design & Embedded Systems** - 18 seats
3. **Digital Communication** - 18 seats
4. **Power Electronics** - 18 seats
5. **Machine Design** - 18 seats
6. **Structural Engineering** - 18 seats
7. **Environmental Engineering** - 18 seats

### MBA (Master of Business Administration)
- **Intake**: 120 seats
- **Specializations**: Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations
- **Eligibility**: Any graduate with valid MAT/CMAT/KMAT score
- **Fee**: ₹80,000 per year (approximate)

### MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
- **Intake**: 60 seats
- **Duration**: 2 years
- **Eligibility**: BCA/B.Sc with Mathematics
- **Focus**: Advanced Programming, Software Development, Project Management
