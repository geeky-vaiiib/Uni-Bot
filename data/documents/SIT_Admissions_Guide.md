# SIT Admissions Guide

## Admission Process for B.E./B.Tech

### Through Karnataka CET (KCET)
- **College Code**: E16
- **Counseling**: Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA)
- **Process**:
  1. Appear for KCET examination (April-May)
  2. Results announced in June
  3. Document verification
  4. Online counseling and seat selection
  5. Fee payment and admission confirmation

### Through COMEDK UGET
- **College Code**: E125
- **Process**:
  1. Register on COMEDK website
  2. Appear for COMEDK UGET (May)
  3. Counseling based on rank
  4. Document verification and admission

### Management Quota
- **Seats**: 15% of total intake
- **Process**: Direct application to college
- **Selection**: Based on 12th marks and entrance test

### NRI/Foreign Nationals
- **Seats**: 5% of total intake
- **Fee Structure**: Higher than regular seats
- **Documents Required**: Passport, Visa, NRI status proof

## Eligibility Criteria

### For B.E. Programs
- **10+2**: Pass with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
- **Minimum Marks**: 45% in PCM (40% for reserved categories)
- **Age**: No upper age limit
- **Entrance**: Valid CET/COMEDK/JEE Main score

### For B.Arch Program
- **10+2**: Pass with Mathematics
- **NATA**: Valid NATA score required
- **Additional**: Aptitude test may be conducted

### For M.Tech Programs
- **Eligibility**: B.E./B.Tech in relevant branch with 50% marks
- **Preference**: Valid GATE score
- **Selection**: GATE score + Interview

### For MBA
- **Eligibility**: Any graduate degree with 50% marks
- **Entrance**: Valid MAT/CMAT/KMAT/PGCET score
- **Selection**: Entrance score + GD + Interview

### For MCA
- **Eligibility**: BCA/B.Sc./B.Com with Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation level
- **Entrance**: Karnataka PGCET
- **Minimum Marks**: 50% in graduation

## Scholarships

### SIT Scholarship Scheme
- **CET Rank ≤ 1000 (B.E.)**: 100% tuition fee waiver for 1st year
- **CET Rank ≤ 25 (B.Arch)**: 100% tuition fee waiver for 1st year
- **Continuation**: Maintain CGPA ≥ 8.5 for subsequent years

### Government Scholarships
1. **SC/ST Scholarship**: Full fee reimbursement
2. **OBC Scholarship**: Partial fee waiver
3. **Minority Scholarship**: For eligible students
4. **National Scholarship Portal**: Various central schemes

### Merit Scholarships
- Top 3 rankers in each branch
- Sports achievers
- Olympiad qualifiers
